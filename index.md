# Privacy Policy for Nomado Mobile App

**Last updated:** Jul 19, 2025

## Information We Collect

### Location Information
- We collect your location data to show nearby activities and provide location-based services
- Location data is only used when the app is actively being used
- You can disable location services in your device settings

### User Data
- Profile information you provide (interests, dietary preferences)
- Saved activities and favorites
- App usage analytics to improve user experience

### Device Information
- Device type and operating system
- App version and crash reports
- Performance metrics

## How We Use Your Information

- Provide location-based activity recommendations
- Save your preferences and favorites
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Send notifications about nearby activities (with your permission)

## Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored securely on our servers
- We use industry-standard encryption
- We do not sell your personal information to third parties

## Your Rights

- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of location tracking
- Contact us with privacy concerns
